A North Queensland Icon

Fast becoming one of North Queensland’s cultural icons, the Barrier Reef Orchestra this year launches its 17th year of performing orchestral music through North Queensland. BRO is a community Orchestra consisting of some of the finest hand-picked musicians not just from Townsville, but as far afield as Cairns and Mackay.
