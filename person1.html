Alan Nixon
Life Member since May 2015

Alan has been the Secretary for the Barrier Reef Orchestra for upwards of eight years.

He is a former Commonwealth Government officer, mainly specialising in the Commonwealth Employment Service. Prior to this he was the Department's Zone manager for North Queensland.

This specialised expertise has greatly helped the Orchestra, facilitating the North Queensland Ensembles to become incorporated.

Alan has an extensive background in community service, having been a member of both the Australian Jaycees and Rotary. On top of this, he has had experience as an actor, producer and Committee member with several smaller theatrical organisations.
