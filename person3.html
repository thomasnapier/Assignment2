David Pearse
Life Member since June 2006

David was heavily involved in the Towsnville Music scene in the 1970s, including the formation of the Barrier Reef Orchestra in 1999.

He was made a Life Member by the committee in June 2006, making him one of the oldest members in the Orchestra.

Him and his wife, Elizabeth, left Townsville to live in Canberra.
