Mario Dobernig
Conductor

Conductor Mario Dobernig grew up in a small village in the Austraian Alps before establishing his professional career as a percussionist in Graz and Helsinki, where he worked extensively with many chamber Orchestras and ensembles such as the Graz Opera, Finnish Radio Symphony Orchestra and Tapiola Sinfonietta.

Mario graduated from the Art University Graz and the Sibelius Academy Helsinki.
