Yoshika Masuda
Cello

Born in Kobe, Japan, Yoshika Masuda first began playing the cello at the age of 5. Following studies with George Pedersen in Australia, he went onto study at the Royal Northern College of Music in Manchester on a full scholarship. Yoshika gained his Bachelor of Music with first class honours from the RNCM, recieving both the Sir John Munduell Principals' Prize and the Leonard Rose Cello Award for outstanding achievement. He then went on to complete his Masters in Music at the RNCM with a distinction the following year.

Yoshika has performed at numerous concerts and recitals throughout Australia, Austria, France, Italy, Japan, the UK and USA. He is currently continuing his studies in Los Angeles as a Doctorate student.
