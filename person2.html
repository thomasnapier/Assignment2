Carol Dall'Osto
Life Member since July 2010

Carol has been a staple part of the Orchestra for many years despite living in Ingham until the early 2000's, when she moved to Townsville.

Carol is a piano teacher, accompanist, and musical director of the Australian Concerto and Vocal Competition which she has managed since 1991.

She was involved with others in the formation of the Barrier Reef Orchestra, and was the chairsperson of the North Queensland Ensembles committee which she maintained until 2003.
